# Invariants

## Architecture
- Use MVVM only
- Do not propose VIPER, Redux, Clean Architecture unless explicitly approved

## Tech Stack
- iOS: Swift + SwiftUI
- Backend: Kotlin + Ktor
- Use minimum dependencies
- Use only free APIs

## Technical Decisions
- Networking through URLSession on iOS
- No heavy DI frameworks
- Local persistence preferred over cloud-first solutions

## Business Rules
- MVP must fit in 2 weeks
- Team size is 4 people
- Solutions must be realistic for MVP scope
